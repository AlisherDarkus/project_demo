This topic contains a set of rules that every subscriber or guest of this public must obey, regardless of whether he is in the manual or not. However, for convenience, the rules for senior positions and ordinary subscribers will be described separately.

RULES!

The user has the right:

  • To offer his fights.
  • In a cultural form, comment on the posted profiles. Swearing is allowed, but not encouraged.
  • Attach photos, videos and / or audio recordings to comments, if they do not contain insults of any form, profuse obscenities, do not have sexual content.

The subscriber has no right to:

  • Insult other people, characters, universes, profiles, this project and its friends, religions, nations and so on in the comments.
  • Attach photos, videos and / or audio recordings containing insults, profuse obscenities, with sexual content to comments.
  • Leave messages in threads.
  • Flood and unnecessarily offtopic in the comments.


The editor has the right to:

  • Freely post their fights.
  • Freely leave blanks for your fights in delayed news.
  • Update the list of hashtags.
  • Issue warnings to violators of the rules and ban those who do not heed them (the ban is made with exclusion from the community).
  • Fill in some topics (by agreement with the administrator)

The editor has no right to:

  • Publish proposed fights if they do not meet the criteria.
  • Post/delete proposed fights without first discussing them with other management members.
  • Knowledge only from own universEdit the fights of other members of the leadership without the knowledge of the authore.
  • Edit / delete pending news without the knowledge of the author.
  • Delete / change themes without the consent of the administrator.
  • Flood and unnecessarily offtopic in the comments.
  • Each of the editors knows certain areas well, and the discussion of battles is often carried out by the whole team. If necessary, the author will be asked questions in private messages. A rejected fight is non-negotiable.

Penalties for the subscriber:

  • For any misconduct, except for insulting projects or management, a warning is issued, if ignored, a 2-day ban is issued.
  • In these cases, a 2-day ban is immediately issued.
  • In case of repetition of the incident without warning, a ban for a week follows.
  • In case of repetition of the incident without warning should be banned for a month.
  • After the fourth violation, a ban up to a permanent one follows.
